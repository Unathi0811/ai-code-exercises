## Principles Applied

All exercises follow these key principles:

1. **Communicate Clearly**: Provide specific, contextual, structured, descriptive prompts
2. **Verify**: Always verify AI responses through testing and human review
3. **Learning Through Guided Practice**: Use AI as a guide, not a code generator
4. **Know Your Words**: Understand terminology to communicate effectively
5. **Drive Your Own Learning**: Be an active learner, use AI to support learning
